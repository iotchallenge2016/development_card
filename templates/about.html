About Parkify

Life has become faster while parking continues to be a slowdown in our daily lives. Looking for a parking spot takes not only time, but also gas of driving around in circles in hopes of finding a good one. This is where Parkify comes in: an application which helps you manage your parking lot by areas, helping not only you, but also the drivers and the climate.

Parkify divides your parking lot in different zones and gives you a mobile app which monitors how many vehicles enter and exit each of this zones. This way, you will be able to have better management over your lot while giving your customer a better experience. The less driving around, the more time that is saved and the less gas that is wasted; help yourself and your customers find a better spot.
